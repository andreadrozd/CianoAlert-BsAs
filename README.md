## Cyanobacteria Alert by CyanoSat
## Buenos Aires
### Weekly Updates
Sentinel-2 and Sentinel-3 imagery is processed on a weekly basis to estimate near-infrared backscatter associated with bacterial-type cells, photosynthetic pigments, and complementary cyanobacterial pigments. By integrating these variables, indicators of risk level and weekly trends are estimated.
